About Me

Hi, I'm Hayley ♡

What I Offer

Treatments

Lip Blush

Lip blush is a permanent makeup tattoo treatment that enhances the natural colour, shape and definition of your lips by only tattooing on to lip tissue (not onto face tissue).

I do 2 different styles of lip blush; a sheer lip tint effect and a lip stick effect. Both are the same process however with a lip stick effect blush, I am implanting more pigment into your lips which gives a more saturated lip stick effect.

From £250

Brows

Hyper-realism brows, powder brows and combination brows are permanent makeup tattoo treatments that can create, define and fill your brows depending on your brow goals, your starting point and your maintenance goals.

Hyper-realism brows are hair-stroke brows, powder brows give a similar result to a brow tint and combination brows are some hair-strokes and some shading. All 3 styles can be very natural and/or more bold depending on your style and your goals.

From £225
